Setting:

January 10th, 1969. President George Wallace gives his State of the Union Address. He congratulates the country on its ability to stamp out Communism. He celebrates the largest American work camp built in California. Meanwhile, many minorities are round up and either deported if possible, imprisoned if needed, or sent straight to works camps that now dot the landscape. Fascism has effectively won over the American people and its politicians. There is hope though.

Over the years post WWII people started seeing the writing on the walls. Literally, fascist propaganda covered walls, played in theaters, and was blasted over the air waves. Those who feared for their lives started taking to the streets in protests and riots. When it was clear that wouldn't be enough many groups started stockpiling food, water, and weapons.

The Resistance generally refers to anyone apart of the fight against the government. There are sub groups within, but some just refer to themselves as being apart of The Resistance. Gun running, moonshining, espionage. These are but some of the jobs done by underground resistance members. All orders come from anonymous leaders who base their operations out of Seattle Washington. The highest leader and starter of the modern movement is simply referred to as Leader or L for short.
