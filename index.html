Introduction:

Welcome. This wiki has been made to organize the world built for a GUPRS campaign my friends and I are running. Inside is an alternative history taking place in 1960s USA. The point of change mostly starts at the successful assassination of Hitler during WWII. The material here is meant for a mature audience only as there are themes and words that may be offensive. Despite being for a private game I am taking care to research and ensure I handle things realistically and tactfully.

Setting:

January 10th, 1969. President George Wallace gives his State of the Union Address. He congratulates the country on its ability to stamp out Communism. He celebrates the largest American work camp built in California. Meanwhile, many minorities are round up and either deported if possible, imprisoned if needed, or sent straight to works camps that now dot the landscape. Fascism has effectively won over the American people and its politicians. There is hope though.

Over the years post WWII people started seeing the writing on the walls. Literally, fascist propaganda covered walls, played in theaters, and was blasted over the air waves. Those who feared for their lives started taking to the streets in protests and riots. When it was clear that wouldn't be enough many groups started stockpiling food, water, and weapons.

The Resistance generally refers to anyone apart of the fight against the government. There are sub groups within, but some just refer to themselves as being apart of The Resistance. Gun running, moonshining, espionage. These are but some of the jobs done by underground resistance members. All orders come from anonymous leaders who base their operations out of Seattle Washington. The highest leader and starter of the modern movement is simply referred to as Leader or L for short.

Timeline:


    1944: Operation Valkyrie is successful in killing Adolf Hitler. The explosion killed Hitler instantly and three men. However, the surviving Nazi operatives within the room managed to relay a message to the capital, stopping the coup members from blaming the Nazi's as intended. The men involved were put to death by firing squad. With Hitler dead, the German army is crippled to the point that they can't properly control a western and eastern front. The sue for peace with the west in exchange for an early offensive against the growing power of Russia.

    1945: The United States drops the first two atomic bombs in Japan, causing an almost immediate surrender.

    1952: [Camp Stonewall] is built in Clarksburg, West Virginia. This marks the first of what would eventually be 26 work camps within the United States

    1953: President Thurmond signs the Sanctity of Marriage Bill into law, banning homosexual and interracial marriage on a federal level. Protests and riots break out across the states. The national guard is called in to quell these outbursts.

    1954: President Thurmond signs the Purity Bill into law, banning all forms of sexual acts that are not purely vaginal. More riots and conflicts happen between national guard and citizens.

    1956: Due to the rising tension among the populous, the KKK is officially recruited as the [Aspro Organization] (AO). Some members decided to remain purely as the Klan, though all help the AO when called upon. The AO itself is ran by [Theophilus Eugene "Bull" Connor].
