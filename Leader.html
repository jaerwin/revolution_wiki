Leader

Leader is the head of The Resistance. They rarely reveal themselves to anyone but the closest of allies. They have worked hard to gather some of the best free thinkers, sympathetic politicians, and ex military leaders to join in the fight against what has become a fascist US government.

They were born to to an African-American father and a Jewish immigrant mother on December 12th, 1930. They grew up in a small Midwestern town were they were treated mostly with kindness. However, school they went to was segregated and only allowed white people into the main library. The local town ran library was one of the few desegregated places around. The local librarian helped with school work while also letting them check out any book they want. At the age of 12 they dropped out of school to begin working to help keep up with bills and their mothers growing medical expenses. Though this didn't stop them from continuing to read at the local library every night.

Fascinated by history they read books on ancient Rome, Greece, Egypt. They were fascinated by the politics and philosophies of the time and started to see the connections to the nation they lived in. They started to read into military tactics and diplomacy, aspiring to lead their country to be better than what it was today. It was also in this library they met Richard Donald who with his help would be the start of The Resistance.
